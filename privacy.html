Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026

Introduction

BolsterMed LLC ("BolsterMed," "we," "us," or "our") operates the bolstermed.app platform, which facilitates professional reference surveys for healthcare hiring organizations. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

From Hiring Organizations: Account credentials (email, password), organization name, and candidate information submitted through our admin dashboard.

From References: Name, email address, phone number (provided by the hiring organization), and survey responses submitted through our platform.

From Candidates: Name, role, department, and other professional details as provided by the hiring organization.

Automatically Collected: Device type, browser type, IP address, and usage data when you interact with our platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to deliver and operate our reference survey platform, send transactional communications (email and SMS survey invitations, reminders), generate reference reports for hiring organizations, improve and maintain our services, and comply with legal obligations.

SMS/Text Messaging

BolsterMed may send SMS messages to references on behalf of hiring organizations. These messages contain survey invitation links and are transactional in nature. Message frequency is limited — typically one initial invitation and up to two reminders per reference request. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for support.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances: survey responses are shared with the hiring organization that initiated the reference request; we use third-party service providers (such as email and SMS delivery services) to operate our platform; and we may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), secure database storage with row-level security, and access controls limiting data visibility to authorized users.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Hiring organizations may request deletion of their data by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the address below. References may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a revised effective date.
